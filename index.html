What is Plate Lunch Digest?

Every morning, we scour dozens of local sources, Hawaii News Now, Civil Beat, Pacific Business News, Star Advertiser, and more, so you don't have to.

We distill the most important stories into a quick, scannable digest that lands in your inbox before your first cup of coffee. Business moves. Real estate trends. Clean energy developments. Tourism updates. All the stories shaping Hawaii, in one place.

Like its namesake, Plate Lunch Digest is hearty, balanced, and satisfying, a complete meal of news without the filler. Read it in 5 minutes and start your day informed.

About

Plate Lunch Digest was born from a simple frustration: keeping up with Hawaii news meant juggling a dozen different sources every morning. We built this digest because we wanted something better, a single, well curated briefing that covers what matters without the noise.

Every day, we read through Hawaii News Now, Civil Beat, Pacific Business News, the Star Advertiser, and more so you don't have to. We distill the most important stories into a quick read that respects your time.

The name? It's a nod to Hawaii's most iconic meal, diverse ingredients, brought together on one plate. That's what we do with news. Business, real estate, energy, tourism, all in one place, every morning.
